1)Positive news Section

In light of recent events,when we mostly hear negative news (like rise in cases) ,so we at 'The Positive Side' have decided to spread positive news. Being positive is the need of hour,so that we can work effectively.we completely acknowledge the fact that constantly getting across negative news can have an adverse effect on your mental health . Getting to know about what is happening around us is also extremely important and hence we can't just stop listening to news. A solution to this problem as suggested by the experts is to maintain a ratio of 4:1 i.e for every 4 negative news you read , look for 1 positive news . This will help you to stay calm and to cope up with these testing times.That is why we have created a section for positive news which will help y'all to get going with these challenging situations. we recognise that you must be stressed and might need an escape from your day to day activities.
